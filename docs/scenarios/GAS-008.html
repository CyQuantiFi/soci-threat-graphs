GAS-008 · Wiper malware destroying control-room historian and engineering data

Destructive wiper malware is deployed in the control room and destroys the data historian, engineering documentation, configuration backups, and operator workstations. OT control continues but the operator loses the ability to investigate the event, demonstrate operating envelopes to the regulator, or restore systems quickly. Reflects the documented use of wiper malware against critical infrastructure since the Shamoon attacks 2012-2017 and ongoing activity by destructive-malware-capable state actors.
