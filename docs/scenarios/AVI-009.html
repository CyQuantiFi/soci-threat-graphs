AVI-009 · Air-cargo screening equipment tampering enabling prohibited-item carriage

An attacker compromises the cargo-screening equipment management network at an airport — explosive trace detection units, X-ray units, container scanning systems — and tampers with calibration or detection sensitivity to allow prohibited items to pass undetected. Pattern reflects ICAO and DHA aviation-security concerns about cyber risk to screening technology and the documented Office of Inspector General reports on screening equipment elsewhere.
