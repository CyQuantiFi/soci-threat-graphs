ELE-003 · AMI head-end system exploitation for mass metering manipulation

An attacker compromises the Advanced Metering Infrastructure (AMI) head-end system and gains the ability to issue commands to a large population of smart meters — including remote-disconnect commands and tariff or billing data manipulation. Mass-disconnect is the higher-consequence path; data manipulation is the higher-probability path. AMI head-end exposure is consistently flagged in AESCSF guidance and in academic research on the meter ecosystem.
