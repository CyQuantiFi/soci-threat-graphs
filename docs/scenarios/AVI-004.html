AVI-004 · Passenger biometric and ePassport kiosk compromise leaking PII at scale

An attacker compromises the airport's biometric and ePassport processing platform — including SmartGate or equivalent kiosks — and exfiltrates biometric templates, passport details, and travel history of large numbers of passengers over a sustained period. No operational disruption, but extreme privacy and national-security consequence. Anchored to ongoing concerns at the Department of Home Affairs about biometric platform security and to OAIC NDB statistics on PII at airports.
