POR-005 · Bunker-fuel logistics system compromise enabling fuel-quantity fraud

An attacker compromises the operator's bunker-fuel logistics and metering system to either falsify delivered-fuel quantities (defrauding shippers or operators) or to disrupt fuel supply to bunkering vessels. Targets the well-documented weakness of OT-protocol-speaking custody-transfer systems and ENISA-flagged maritime fuel logistics risk.
