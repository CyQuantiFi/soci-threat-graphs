ELE-008 · Edge firewall CVE mass-exploitation establishing persistent access

Mass exploitation of an edge firewall or remote-access appliance vulnerability (FortiOS, PAN-OS, Ivanti, Cisco) establishes persistent access in the operator's corporate network. The attacker remains dormant for weeks while studying the IT/OT boundary, then escalates as opportunity allows (extortion, sabotage, or onward sale of access). Anchored to CISA AA24-038A and the pattern of cyber-criminal access brokerage observed in the AU energy sector 2023-2025.
