ELE-005 · Cloud-hosted renewable-inverter management platform compromised at scale

An attacker compromises the cloud-hosted management platform used by a large-inverter or VPP aggregator to control distributed solar, wind, or battery assets. Adversary commands inverters to switch off or to inject reactive power outside normal operating envelopes, with stability implications for the local distribution network. Pattern anchored to AEMO and ENA research on cyber risks to consumer energy resources and to disclosed vulnerabilities in inverter cloud platforms 2022-2025.
