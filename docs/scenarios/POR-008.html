POR-008 · DDoS and protocol abuse against terminal gate system halting truck flow

An attacker uses a DDoS attack combined with targeted protocol abuse to bring down the truck-gate system at a major container terminal. Truck flow stops, road queues form, and yard storage capacity saturates within hours. Pattern reflects the documented DDoS-for-extortion campaigns observed in AU and globally 2022-2025 and the cascading capacity problems at port-yard scale.
