HLT-009 · Pharmacy automated dispensing system tampering creating medication errors

An attacker compromises the hospital pharmacy automated dispensing system and alters drug-to-bin mappings or dose limits, causing medication errors at scale until clinical staff notice via incident reports. Distinct from HLT-004 (which targets pumps directly) because this scenario sits at the pharmacy-to-ward boundary. Pattern reflects HC3 advisories on automated dispensing cabinets and TGA cybersecurity concerns about pharmacy automation.
