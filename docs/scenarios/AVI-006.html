AVI-006 · Aviation fuel hydrant system tampering disrupting refuelling

An attacker compromises the aviation-fuel hydrant control system at a major airport, either denying fuel to aircraft during turnaround or manipulating quantity data to cause weight-and-balance discrepancies. Anchored to the 2022 cyber attack on the Mabanaft / Oiltanking fuel distribution group in Germany which affected supply to several major airports and to general concerns about fuel-supply cyber risk noted by IATA.
