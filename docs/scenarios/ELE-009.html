ELE-009 · Insider exfiltration of grid topology and protection-settings data

A current or recently-departed engineer with access to grid topology, protection settings, and SCADA configuration exfiltrates this data to an external party (state-aligned actor, broker, or competitor). The data itself does not cause an outage but enables a subsequent attacker to craft a much more precise and damaging operation against the network. Pattern documented in ASIO and CERT Australia advisories on espionage targeting AU energy operators.
