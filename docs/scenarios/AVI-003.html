AVI-003 · Air-cargo terminal management ransomware disrupting freight handling

Ransomware encrypts the cargo-management system at an airport freight terminal, halting documentation, customs interface, and warehouse movements. Perishable and time-sensitive cargo (medical, e-commerce, pharmaceutical) is most affected. Pattern follows DP World Australia 2023 for the container-port equivalent and the broader pattern of cargo- terminal ransomware events globally.
