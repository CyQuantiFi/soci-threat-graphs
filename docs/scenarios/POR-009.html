POR-009 · Port Community System compromise affecting multiple stakeholders

An attacker compromises a Port Community System (PCS) — the shared data exchange platform between port operators, shipping lines, freight forwarders, customs, and other stakeholders. PCS compromise cascades across many ecosystem participants simultaneously and causes downstream loss disproportionate to a single-operator event. Anchored to ENISA Port Community System cyber guidance and the documented PCS exposure at multiple international ports.
