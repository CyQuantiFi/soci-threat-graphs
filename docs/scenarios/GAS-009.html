GAS-009 · Asset-management data poisoning misdirecting integrity-management programme

An attacker quietly tampers with the gas operator's pipeline asset- management and integrity-management database, altering inline-inspection results, anomaly classifications, or remaining-life calculations. The operator's integrity-management programme — required under AS 2885 — proceeds based on falsified data, missing real defects until they fail in service. A long-dwell tampering scenario consistent with state-actor patience and Dragos's documented industrial-data-targeting tooling.
