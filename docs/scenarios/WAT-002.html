WAT-002 · Enterprise ransomware halting billing, customer service, and asset management

Ransomware encrypts the utility's enterprise IT systems — billing, customer service, asset management, GIS, and the corporate file shares used to issue work orders. SCADA continues to run because of network segmentation, but the operator loses the ability to invoice, respond to customer outages, or dispatch field crews to confirmed faults. The South Staffs Water / Clop incident in 2022, the Aliquippa Pennsylvania water authority intrusion in 2023, and multiple disclosed AU local-government water incidents show the pattern is credible for AU water operators of all sizes.

Suggested controls

  • E8-MFA — Multi-factor authentication for all internet-facing accounts (Essential-Eight)
  • E8-BACKUPS — Daily backups with offline copy and tested recovery (Essential-Eight)
  • PR.DS-1 — Data-at-rest protection including offline backups (NIST-CSF)
  • CIRMP-CYBER-9 — Tested incident response and business continuity plan (SOCI-CIRMP)
