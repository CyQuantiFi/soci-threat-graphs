POR-006 · Vessel Traffic Services disruption causing navigation safety incident

An attacker disrupts the Vessel Traffic Services (VTS) system used to coordinate vessel movements in a port's approaches and confined waters, either by ransomware or targeted protocol abuse on the VTS data feeds (AIS, radar, VHF). Loss of VTS situational awareness forces ships to rely on visual and direct radio coordination, raising collision and grounding risk. Pattern reflects ENISA and IMO-Maritime-Cyber Risk Management guidance on VTS as a high-consequence target.
