GAS-001 · Enterprise ransomware forcing precautionary pipeline shutdown

Ransomware encrypts the gas pipeline operator's enterprise IT environment — billing, scheduling, and the corporate file shares used to coordinate field operations and customer nominations. Although OT systems are not directly affected, the operator initiates a precautionary shutdown of pipeline operations until confidence in IT/OT segmentation is restored. Closely follows the 2021 Colonial Pipeline incident in the US and remains the most-cited reference for cyber-driven pipeline shutdowns.
