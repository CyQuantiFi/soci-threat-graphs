ELE-006 · Generator dispatch and market-bidding interface compromise

An attacker compromises a generation company's bidding and dispatch interface to AEMO — the system that submits offers into the National Electricity Market and receives dispatch instructions. Tampering with bids or dispatch responses can disrupt market clearing, lose the operator revenue, or introduce system-security concerns when responses diverge from instructions. Anchored to AEMO's documented concerns about cyber risk to market-facing systems and AESCSF market-interface controls.
