AVI-002 · Airline Departure Control System ransomware halting passenger processing

Ransomware encrypts the airline-shared Departure Control System (DCS) or common-use passenger-processing platform at an airport (e.g. Amadeus Altea, SITA AirportConnect Open), affecting check-in, bag-drop, boarding-gate operations across multiple airlines simultaneously. Reflects the 2018 Bristow-and-Air-NZ DCS outage pattern and the 2024 Microsoft / CrowdStrike global ground-stop incident — both of which caused mass-cancellation conditions even without targeted attack.
