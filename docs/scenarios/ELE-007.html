ELE-007 · Spear-phishing of control-room operator leading to OT-network access

A targeted spear-phishing campaign aimed at named control-room operators delivers a credential-harvesting page or a remote-access trojan. The attacker obtains operator credentials and uses them through the engineering remote-access pathway to reach the control network. Differs from ELE-001 in that this targets human operators directly via socially-engineered bait — common AU energy-sector incidents 2022-2025 used this vector.
