HLT-003 · Business email compromise driving fraudulent vendor payment

An attacker compromises a finance team email account at a hospital network via phishing, monitors invoice traffic, and impersonates a real medical- equipment vendor to redirect a high-value payment to an attacker-controlled account. Pattern is the highest-frequency cyber financial-fraud event per ACSC ReportCyber statistics and is well documented in AU healthcare.
