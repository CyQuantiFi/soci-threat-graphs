POR-007 · Insider stevedore using terminal systems for cargo misappropriation

A stevedore or terminal-systems user with legitimate access to the Terminal Operating System uses their access to facilitate cargo misappropriation — selectively releasing containers to colluding road hauliers, altering manifests, or covering up the diversion in the audit trail. Pattern is well-documented in AFP and ABF organised-crime investigations and reflects the ongoing concern about insider risk in AU and global port operations.
