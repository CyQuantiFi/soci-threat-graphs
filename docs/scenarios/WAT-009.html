WAT-009 · Customer portal credential stuffing causing mass PII disclosure

An attacker uses credentials from unrelated breaches to access the utility's online customer portal at scale. Successful logins disclose customer names, addresses, payment details, hardship-program participation, and consumption patterns that can be combined with other data sources for downstream fraud. No OT impact, but a notifiable data-breach event under the Privacy Act 1988 and reputational and legal exposure. Anchored to the documented use of credential-stuffing against AU online services 2022-2025 and the OAIC's consistent ranking of credential stuffing in NDB statistics.
