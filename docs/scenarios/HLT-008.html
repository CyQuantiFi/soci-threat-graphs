HLT-008 · ICU patient-monitor network flood disrupting clinical observation

A worm-like infection or deliberate flood overwhelms the network segment serving ICU patient monitors, causing missed alarms, lost telemetry, and delayed clinical response. Pattern reflects the documented WannaCry impact on NHS UK in 2017, where networked medical devices and monitors were severely degraded, and ongoing concerns at the TGA and HC3 about network-flooding effects on safety-critical clinical IoMT.
