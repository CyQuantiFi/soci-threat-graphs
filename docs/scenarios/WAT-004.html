WAT-004 · Insider operator abusing legitimate SCADA credentials to manipulate treatment

A current or recently-departed control engineer with legitimate SCADA access uses their credentials to alter chemical dosing, pump scheduling, or alarm thresholds, either maliciously or as a coercion victim. Loss event is amplified because the access is authorised and bypasses perimeter controls. Anchored to the documented pattern of insider events in critical infrastructure including the 2000 Maroochy Shire wastewater incident in Queensland — still the most-cited case of insider-attributed ICS sabotage in the academic literature.
