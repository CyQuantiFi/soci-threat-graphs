HLT-001 · Hospital ransomware with lateral movement to medical-device subnets

An attacker delivers ransomware into a hospital's enterprise IT environment and moves laterally onto biomedical-engineering and medical-device subnets, where unsupported legacy operating systems and IoT-class imaging devices cannot be patched or run modern agents. Loss of imaging, electronic medical records, and pathology systems forces re-routing of patients and cancellation of elective procedures. Pattern documented in numerous AU and international healthcare incidents 2020-2025.
