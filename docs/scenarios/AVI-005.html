AVI-005 · Ground-handler phishing leading to airside system access

Spear-phishing against a contracted ground-handling provider yields credentials that work on the airport's airside operations systems. The attacker uses these to manipulate load-planning, slot allocation, or turnaround coordination, causing delays and unsafe-load conditions. Pattern reflects the airport-ecosystem multi-employer access model and the documented phishing rates against the AU aviation sector per ACSC.
