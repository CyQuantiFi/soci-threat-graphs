HLT-007 · Insider clinician inappropriate access to VIP and family medical records

A clinician with legitimate EMR access inappropriately views, copies, or discusses the records of high-profile patients (politicians, celebrities, former partners, family members) outside of their care responsibilities. Pattern is the highest-frequency insider event in healthcare per OAIC and AHPRA disciplinary records, and one of the most consistently notifiable categories under the Notifiable Data Breaches scheme.
