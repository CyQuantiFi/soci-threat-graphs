WAT-007 · Dam spillway control compromised via OEM vendor remote-support tunnel

An adversary compromises an OEM vendor's remote-support environment and uses a persistent tunnel into the dam-control SCADA to issue unauthorised spillway or release-valve commands. Even minor unauthorised releases affect downstream flood risk, agricultural users, hydroelectric scheduling, and environmental flow obligations. The 2013 disclosure of a US dam SCADA intrusion (Bowman Avenue Dam, NY) established the scenario in public literature; the AESCSF supply-chain control area treats vendor tunnels as a top-tier risk for water-bulk operators.
