GAS-002 · Compressor station PLC manipulation via vendor remote-support tunnel

An attacker compromises the OEM vendor environment supporting compressor stations along a transmission pipeline, abuses the vendor's persistent remote-support tunnel to issue unauthorised commands to a compressor PLC, and either trips the station offline or pushes it into an unsafe operating regime. Pattern is documented in CISA AA22-103A on tools targeting ICS/SCADA devices and in Dragos and Mandiant pipeline-sector reports.
