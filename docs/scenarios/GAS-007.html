GAS-007 · Third-party engineering firm compromise as supply-chain entry

An attacker compromises an external engineering consultancy that provides process-engineering services to a gas operator and has read-only access to the operator's control-system documentation, historian data, and a jump host. The attacker uses this trusted-third-party access to pivot into the operator's environment. Reflects the increasing prominence of third-party access as an initial vector in Mandiant M-Trends and the AESCSF supply-chain control area.
