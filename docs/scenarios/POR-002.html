POR-002 · Ship-to-shore crane control tampering disrupting vessel discharge

An attacker reaches the operator workstation network for ship-to-shore (STS) cranes and either prevents operator commands reaching the crane PLCs or alters operating-envelope parameters. Vessel discharge halts and resumes manual control with safety-officer escort, sharply reducing throughput. The 2024 Wall Street Journal and US Government reporting on Chinese-built STS crane cyber risk and the documented Konecranes vulnerabilities anchor the scenario.
