ELE-002 · Distribution Management System ransomware halting outage restoration

Ransomware encrypts the Distribution Management System (DMS) and outage- management workstations used by a distribution network operator. Field switching, restoration coordination, and customer outage notifications fall back to manual or paper-based processes. Pattern matches the documented pressure on AU DNSPs during ransomware incidents 2022-2025 and CISA's ongoing advisories on ransomware in the energy sector.
