HLT-004 · Infusion-pump IoMT compromise enabling unsafe medication delivery

An attacker exploits a known vulnerability in a network-connected infusion pump or its associated drug library management server. The exploit could allow alteration of dose limits, suppression of alarms, or denial of delivery. Pattern reflects multiple FDA and TGA medical-device cybersecurity advisories on infusion pumps and the broader IoMT-security research base.
