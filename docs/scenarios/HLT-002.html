HLT-002 · PACS imaging-system exfiltration and double-extortion ransomware

An attacker reaches the Picture Archiving and Communication System (PACS) and exfiltrates patient imaging and reports before deploying ransomware. PACS data — including faces, identifiers, and diagnoses — is particularly sensitive. The double-extortion model adds publication threat to the encryption event. Anchored to multiple AU and international healthcare PACS ransomware incidents 2022-2025 and OAIC NDB statistics consistently ranking healthcare as the most-affected sector.
