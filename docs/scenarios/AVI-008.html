AVI-008 · Insider airport IT administrator sabotage of common-use systems

A current or recently-departed airport IT administrator with retained privileged access either intentionally damages the common-use platform, changes configurations causing later outages, or exfiltrates sensitive data. Insider sabotage scenarios are well documented in CERT Insider Threat Center publications and the typical aviation-IT environment has many privileged users.
