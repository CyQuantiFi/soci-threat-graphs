ELE-004 · Industroyer-pattern protocol abuse against transmission substations

A capable state or state-aligned actor delivers malware that speaks ICS protocols (IEC 60870-5-101/104, IEC 61850, DNP3) natively to issue unauthorised open commands to transmission-substation breakers, replicating the publicly documented Industroyer (2016) and Industroyer2 (2022) attacks on Ukrainian grid operators. Consequence is supply loss measured in hundreds of MW for the duration of restoration.
