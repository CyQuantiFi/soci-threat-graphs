WAT-005 · Supply-chain compromise of laboratory information system vendor

An attacker compromises the supply chain of a Laboratory Information Management System (LIMS) vendor commonly used by AU water utilities to record and certify drinking-water quality test results. Tampered LIMS data could either falsify a clean test (allowing contaminated water to be released to supply) or generate false positives (forcing unnecessary boil-water notifications). Pattern consistent with SolarWinds 2020 and 3CX 2023 supply-chain compromises adapted to the laboratory-systems space.
