WAT-006 · VPN edge-appliance CVE exploited to pivot from corporate to control network

An attacker exploits a known vulnerability in the utility's perimeter VPN appliance (Fortinet, Ivanti Connect Secure, Cisco ASA, Pulse Secure, or similar) to gain authenticated access into the corporate network, harvests credentials, then uses a poorly-segmented jump host or shared admin account to reach the control network. Anchored to CISA AA24-038A on Ivanti Connect Secure exploitation and the documented exploitation of FortiOS CVEs across CI sectors in 2023-2025.
