HLT-005 · Telehealth platform mass account takeover exposing consultations

An attacker uses credential stuffing and an exploited authentication weakness in a telehealth platform to take over patient and clinician accounts at scale, accessing recorded consultations, prescriptions, and chat messages. Reflects the growth of telehealth in AU post-2020 and the OAIC NDB statistics on health-sector data breaches.
